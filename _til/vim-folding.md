Set the fold method to manual:

`set foldmethod=manual`

- `zf` to create a fold.
- `zo` to open a fold.
- `zc` to close a fold.
- `za` to toggle.
- `zd` to delete a fold.
- `zM` close all folds.
- `zR` open all folds.
- `zE` to delete all folds.
